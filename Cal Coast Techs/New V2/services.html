Website Setup & Support

The software used to create custom websites include HTML and Wordpress sites. An initial setup fee and yearly administration (includes support) fees would be charged as well.


Technology Setup & Support

Your computer(s) and other technical devices can be setup in person and managed remotely or physically, if necessary. An online support ticket portal would be used for IT support requests and a management portal would be used for remote management of the computers and/or mobile devices.


Email Setup & Support

An email provider with assigned account details can be setup and details sent via email. Providers that could be used include Google Apps, Microsoft Office 365, cPanel (usually included with web hosting plan, but is entirely dependent on choice of web host), and other options.
